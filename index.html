Privacy Policy - Hamilton Sentiment Analysis Tool

Effective Date: July 14, 2025
Last Updated: July 14, 2025

1. Purpose and Scope

This application is designed to analyze public sentiment around key civic topics in Hamilton, New Zealand. The tool collects and analyzes publicly available social media content to provide insights that inform local government decision-making and enhance civic engagement.

2. Data Collection

We collect:

  • Public posts and comments from Facebook pages and groups related to Hamilton civic matters
  • Publicly available metadata such as post dates, engagement metrics, and basic demographic information
  • No private or personal information is accessed or stored
  • Only content that is already publicly visible is analyzed

3. Data Usage

Collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Sentiment analysis regarding civic topics and public policy matters
  • Generating aggregate reports on community opinions
  • Providing insights to Hamilton City Council for informed decision-making
  • Identifying trends in public discourse on local issues

4. Data Processing and Analysis

All data processing focuses on:

  • Aggregate sentiment trends rather than individual opinions
  • Topic-based analysis of civic issues
  • Statistical summaries that cannot be traced back to individual users
  • Automated sentiment classification and topic modeling

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Data is stored securely with access limited to authorized research personnel
  • Individual user identifiers are anonymized in analysis reports
  • Raw data is retained only as long as necessary for analysis purposes
  • All data handling complies with New Zealand Privacy Act 2020

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share:

  • Aggregated, anonymized insights with Hamilton City Council
  • Statistical summaries for public reporting on civic engagement
  • Research findings that contribute to improved democratic processes
  • We do not sell, trade, or share individual user data with third parties

7. User Rights

As we only process publicly available information:

  • Users can control their privacy through their own Facebook privacy settings
  • Users may contact us to request information about how their public posts are being analyzed
  • Users can request exclusion from future analysis

8. Compliance and Legal Basis

This research operates under:

  • New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 compliance
  • Facebook's Terms of Service and API usage policies
  • Legitimate interest in civic engagement and democratic participation
  • Public interest in transparent and responsive local government

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for:

  • Completing sentiment analysis research
  • Generating reports for civic leaders
  • Ensuring research integrity and reproducibility
  • Typically no longer than 12 months unless required for ongoing civic projects

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through appropriate channels.
